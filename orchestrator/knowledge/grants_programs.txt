GRANTS & FUNDING PROGRAMS FOR WOMEN ENTREPRENEURS IN INDIA
============================================================

1. GOVERNMENT GRANTS — CENTRAL
--------------------------------

a) TECHNOLOGY DEVELOPMENT BOARD (TDB) — WOMEN INNOVATORS
   - Type: Grant + soft loan
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 50 lakh (grant portion 25-50% of project cost)
   - Target: Women innovators commercializing indigenous technology
   - Eligibility: Technology-based enterprises; woman promoter with 51%+ equity
   - Apply: tdb.gov.in

b) BIRAC — WOMEN IN BIOTECH (WiB) PROGRAM
   - Type: Grant
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 50 lakh
   - Target: Women scientists in biotech/life sciences starting ventures
   - Benefits: Mentorship from BIRAC BioIncubators, funding, networking
   - Apply: birac.nic.in

c) NIDHI — PRAYAS (STARTUP INDIA / DST)
   - Type: Grant for prototype development
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 10 lakh per startup
   - Target: Early-stage tech startups (women-led preferred)
   - Apply: Through DST-recognized incubators

d) MSME TECHNOLOGY UPGRADATION — CLCSS
   - Type: Capital subsidy (15% of project cost, max Rs. 15 lakh)
   - Target: Small enterprises including women-led for tech upgradation
   - Apply: Through SIDBI / scheduled commercial banks

e) PM FORMALISATION OF MICRO FOOD PROCESSING ENTERPRISES (PM-FME)
   - Type: Grant (35% of project cost, max Rs. 10 lakh)
   - Target: Women entrepreneurs in food processing
   - SHG members get Rs. 40,000 per member as seed capital
   - Apply: mofpi.gov.in

2. STATE GOVERNMENT GRANTS
----------------------------

a) KERALA — KARMA — WOMEN ENTREPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT
   - Type: Grant + loan
   - Amount: Subsidy up to 25% of project cost
   - Target: Women entrepreneurs in manufacturing and services
   - Apply: Through Kerala Financial Corporation (KFC) / KSIDC

b) MAHARASHTRA — UDYOGINI (Women Industrial Training Centre)
   - Type: Skill training + seed money
   - Target: Women in traditional crafts; BPL families
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 1 lakh seed money with training

c) TAMIL NADU — CHIEF MINISTER'S WOMEN ENTREPRENEUR SCHEME
   - Type: Capital subsidy
   - Amount: 25% subsidy on term loans; max Rs. 75,000
   - Target: Women establishing micro enterprises in Tamil Nadu

d) RAJASTHAN — MAHILA NIDHI (RAJASTHAN WOMEN COOPERATIVE FEDERATION)
   - Type: Micro-finance + grant for SHG women
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 40,000 per SHG member

e) ANDHRA PRADESH — YSR CHEYUTHA
   - Type: Financial assistance
   - Amount: Rs. 75,000 over 4 years to BC, SC, ST, minority women
   - Targeted at: Women aged 45-60 in economic activities

3. INCUBATORS & ACCELERATORS FOR WOMEN
----------------------------------------

a) WE HUB — TELANGANA (INDIA'S FIRST STATE-LED INCUBATOR FOR WOMEN)
   - Services: Office space, mentorship, funding access, legal support
   - Funding facilitated: Rs. 2-5 crore for portfolio startups
   - Apply: wehub.org.in

b) WADHWANI FOUNDATION — WOMEN ENTREPRENEURS PROGRAM
   - Mentorship + funding access for women entrepreneurs
   - Free program; venture support

c) NASSCOM 10,000 STARTUPS — WOMEN TRACK
   - Incubation, mentorship, investor connects
   - Special cohorts for women-led startups

d) AIC — NITI AAYOG ATAL INCUBATION CENTERS
   - 68+ incubators nationwide
   - Grants up to Rs. 10 crore to incubators; startups get subsidized support
   - Many have women-specific programs
   - Apply: aim.gov.in/aic.php

e) IIT/IIM INCUBATORS
   - IIMA CIIE: Funding + mentorship; women entrepreneurs welcome
   - IIT Madras Research Park: Tech startups
   - NSRCEL (IIM Bangalore): Goldman Sachs 10,000 Women program

f) VILLGRO — SOCIAL ENTERPRISE INCUBATOR
   - Focus: Healthcare, agriculture, education social enterprises
   - Grants + equity funding for early-stage startups
   - Women-led enterprises get priority in some programs

4. PRIVATE & CSR GRANTS
-------------------------

a) GOLDMAN SACHS 10,000 WOMEN
   - Type: Business education + funding access
   - Target: Women entrepreneurs globally (including India)
   - Duration: Free business management program (online via Coursera)
   - Certification + alumni network + funding connections
   - Apply: goldmansachs.com/10000women

b) CARTIER WOMEN'S INITIATIVE
   - Type: Global grant competition
   - Amount: USD 100,000 (regional winner), USD 30,000 (fellows)
   - Target: Women-run social impact startups
   - Apply: cartierwomensinitiative.com

c) TATA SOCIAL ENTERPRISE CHALLENGE
   - Type: Prize money + incubation
   - Amount: Rs. 15 lakh prize money
   - Target: Social enterprises (women-led preferred)
   - Apply: tsec.tata.com

d) YES BANK TRANSFORMATION SERIES
   - Funding and mentorship for impactful businesses
   - Women-led categories highlighted

e) HDFC PARIVARTAN GRANTS
   - CSR grants for women-focused social enterprises
   - Amount: Project-based

f) FACEBOOK ACCELERATOR (META) FOR SMALL BUSINESS
   - Free training, tools, and advertising credits for small businesses
   - Women's specific programs periodically launched

5. ANGEL & EQUITY FUNDING NETWORKS FOR WOMEN
----------------------------------------------

a) INDIAN ANGEL NETWORK (IAN) — WOMEN'S COHORT
   - Angel funding for early-stage startups
   - Special interest in women-led ventures

b) VENTURE CATALYSTS — WOMEN ENTREPRENEURS FUND
   - Pre-seed and seed stage funding
   - Active in India's top cities

c) LET'S VENTURE
   - Online platform connecting women founders with angels
   - Apply: letsventure.com

d) SEQUOIA SPARK (NOW PEAK XV)
   - Program for women entrepreneurs
   - Seed funding + mentorship from Sequoia

e) MAHINDRA RISE FOR GOOD — WOMEN EMPOWERMENT GRANTS
   - CSR-based grants for women-led social enterprises

6. INTERNATIONAL GRANTS ACCESSIBLE FROM INDIA
-----------------------------------------------

a) CHERIE BLAIR FOUNDATION — MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
   - Free 12-month mentoring for women entrepreneurs
   - Apply: cherieblairfoundation.org

b) UNFPA — WOMEN ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT GRANTS
   - For NGOs and social enterprises working on women's economic rights

c) WELLS FOR ZOÉ / FORD FOUNDATION
   - Grants for women-focused social enterprises

7. CROWDFUNDING PLATFORMS FOR WOMEN ENTREPRENEURS
--------------------------------------------------

a) KETTO — Popular in India for social impact fundraising
b) MILAAP — Focused on livelihood and social causes
c) RANG DE — P2P lending for micro-entrepreneurs
d) FAIRCENT — P2P lending platform
e) KICKSTARTER / INDIEGOGO — For product startups

APPLICATION TIPS:
- Always register on myscheme.gov.in for government schemes
- Keep documents ready: Aadhaar, PAN, bank account, business registration, caste certificate (if applicable)
- For DPIIT Startup recognition: startupindia.gov.in
- Check eligibility for multiple schemes; many can be combined
- Connect with local District Industries Centre (DIC) for state schemes
- Join women SHGs for group-based funding access
