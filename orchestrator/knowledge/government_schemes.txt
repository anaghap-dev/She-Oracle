GOVERNMENT SCHEMES FOR WOMEN IN INDIA — COMPREHENSIVE GUIDE
=============================================================

1. PM MUDRA YOJANA (PMMY) — MICRO-ENTERPRISE LOANS
----------------------------------------------------
Type: Loan scheme (not grant)
Target: Women entrepreneurs starting or expanding micro enterprises
Loan Categories:
  - Shishu: Up to Rs. 50,000 (for startups, new businesses)
  - Kishore: Rs. 50,001 to Rs. 5 lakhs (for growing businesses)
  - Tarun: Rs. 5 lakhs to Rs. 10 lakhs (for established businesses)
Eligibility: Any woman with a viable business plan; no collateral required for Shishu/Kishore.
Interest Rate: Fixed by lending institution, typically 8.5%-12% per annum.
How to Apply: Visit any public sector bank, private bank, RRB, MFI, or NBFC; apply online at mudra.org.in
Documents: Aadhaar card, business plan, photos, address proof, caste certificate (if applicable).
Special benefit: Women borrowers get 0.25% interest rate concession in many banks.
Success stories: Widely used for tailoring, food processing, beauty parlors, small retail.

2. STAND UP INDIA SCHEME
--------------------------
Type: Loan scheme
Target: SC/ST and women entrepreneurs setting up greenfield enterprises (manufacturing, services, trading).
Loan Amount: Rs. 10 lakhs to Rs. 1 crore.
Eligibility: At least 51% shareholding by woman/SC/ST entrepreneur; first-time enterprise.
Repayment: Up to 7 years with 18-month moratorium.
How to Apply: standupmitra.in portal or any scheduled commercial bank branch.
Support: Handholding support via SIDBI, NABARD, and DIPP.

3. STARTUP INDIA — DPIIT RECOGNITION
--------------------------------------
Type: Recognition + benefits program
Target: Innovative startups (including women-led startups).
Benefits:
  - Tax exemption for 3 years out of first 10 years.
  - Self-certification under 6 labor laws and 3 environmental laws.
  - Fast-track patent application (80% discount on fees).
  - Government procurement preference.
  - Rs. 10,000 crore Fund of Funds via SIDBI.
Eligibility: Incorporated as Private Limited Company, LLP, or Partnership; up to 10 years old; annual turnover < Rs. 100 crore; working on innovative solution.
How to Apply: startupindia.gov.in → Register → Apply for DPIIT Recognition.

4. WOMEN ENTREPRENEURSHIP PLATFORM (WEP)
------------------------------------------
Type: Government platform (NITI Aayog initiative)
Target: Women entrepreneurs at all stages.
Services: Mentorship, funding access, market access, compliance support, incubation.
How to Access: wep.gov.in — free registration.
Features: 1-on-1 mentorship, masterclasses, startup challenges, networking.

5. PM VISHWAKARMA SCHEME
--------------------------
Type: Grant + skill development
Target: Artisans and craftspeople (including women in traditional crafts like weaving, pottery, tailoring).
Benefits:
  - Rs. 15,000 toolkit grant (free).
  - Skill training (basic: 5-7 days; advanced: 15 days) with Rs. 500/day stipend.
  - Collateral-free loans: Rs. 1 lakh (Year 1), Rs. 2 lakhs (Year 2) at 5% interest.
  - Market linkage support.
Eligibility: Self-employed artisan/craftsperson working with hands; 18 trades covered.
How to Apply: pmvishwakarma.gov.in or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).

6. ANNAPURNA SCHEME
---------------------
Type: Loan scheme
Target: Women entrepreneurs in food catering business.
Loan Amount: Up to Rs. 50,000 (no collateral for small amounts).
Purpose: Purchase utensils, equipment for catering business.
Interest: Subsidized rates through State Bank of Mysore and other banks.

7. STREE SHAKTI PACKAGE (STATE BANK OF INDIA)
----------------------------------------------
Type: Loan scheme
Target: Women owning > 50% of enterprise.
Loan Amount: Varies; higher loan limits for women.
Benefit: 0.05% concession in interest rate for loans above Rs. 2 lakhs.
Training: Free EDP (Entrepreneurship Development Programme) training.
Eligibility: Enrolled in state government's Entrepreneurship Development Programme.

8. MAHILA UDYAM NIDHI SCHEME (PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK)
-----------------------------------------------------
Type: Loan + equity scheme
Target: Women entrepreneurs for small scale industries.
Loan Amount: Up to Rs. 10 lakhs.
Repayment: Up to 10 years.
Purpose: Setting up or upgrading small enterprises.

9. CENTRAL SECTOR SCHEME FOR WOMEN — RASHTRIYA MAHILA KOSH (RMK)
------------------------------------------------------------------
Type: Micro-credit facility
Target: Women in informal sector, low-income groups.
Loan Amount: Up to Rs. 1 lakh through NGOs/intermediaries.
Interest Rate: 8% per annum (subsidized).

10. PRADHAN MANTRI AWAS YOJANA (PMAY) — WOMEN COMPONENT
---------------------------------------------------------
Type: Housing subsidy
Benefit: Mandatory co-ownership requirement benefits women; credit-linked subsidy.
EWS/LIG: Interest subsidy of 6.5% on loan up to Rs. 6 lakhs.
MIG-I: 4% interest subsidy on loan up to Rs. 9 lakhs.
MIG-II: 3% interest subsidy on loan up to Rs. 12 lakhs.
Note: Property must be registered in woman's name (or jointly) for EWS/LIG.

11. SUKANYA SAMRIDDHI YOJANA
------------------------------
Type: Savings scheme for girl child
Interest Rate: 8.2% per annum (as of 2024, revised quarterly).
Investment: Min Rs. 250 / year; Max Rs. 1.5 lakh / year.
Tax benefit: Fully exempt under Section 80C and on maturity.
Eligibility: Parents/guardians can open for girl child up to age 10.
Maturity: On girl reaching 21 years or marriage after 18.

12. BETI BACHAO BETI PADHAO
------------------------------
Type: Awareness + convergence scheme
Focus: Improving sex ratio, girls' education and welfare.
Financial benefits: Linked with Sukanya Samriddhi for saving encouragement.
Districts: Special focus on 640 districts across India.

13. NIRBHAYA FUND INITIATIVES
-------------------------------
Type: Safety infrastructure fund
Programs funded: Safe City programme, emergency response system (112), CCTVs in public spaces, one-stop centres for survivors.
One-Stop Centres: Available in 700+ districts; provide medical, legal, psychological support to survivors of violence.

14. NATIONAL RURAL LIVELIHOODS MISSION (DAY-NRLM) — MAHILA KISAN SASHAKTIKARAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Type: Livelihood + SHG support
Target: Rural women farmers.
Benefits: Formation of Self Help Groups (SHGs); bank linkage; skill training; interest subvention on loans (7% effective rate).
Coverage: 9 crore+ women in SHGs nationwide.

15. DIGITAL SAKHI PROGRAMME
------------------------------
Type: Digital literacy
Target: Rural women to become digital entrepreneurs.
Training: Digital payments, e-governance services, mobile banking.
Earning: Sakhi earns commission by providing digital services to villagers.
