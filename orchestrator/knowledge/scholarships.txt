SCHOLARSHIPS & EDUCATION OPPORTUNITIES FOR WOMEN IN INDIA
===========================================================

1. NATIONAL SCHOLARSHIPS PORTAL (NSP) — WOMEN-SPECIFIC
---------------------------------------------------------
Portal: scholarships.gov.in
Key women-focused scholarships:

a) BEGUM HAZRAT MAHAL NATIONAL SCHOLARSHIP (Maulana Azad Foundation)
   - For minority community girls (Class 9-12).
   - Amount: Rs. 5,000 (Class 9-10), Rs. 6,000 (Class 11-12) per year.
   - Eligibility: Minority community (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Zoroastrian); 50%+ marks; family income < Rs. 2 lakh/year.
   - Apply: scholarships.gov.in (July-October annually).

b) PRAGATI SCHOLARSHIP FOR GIRLS (AICTE)
   - For girls pursuing technical education (Degree/Diploma).
   - Amount: Rs. 50,000 per year (tuition + incidentals).
   - Eligibility: 1st year student; family income < Rs. 8 lakh/year; not availing other AICTE scholarship.
   - Apply: aicte-india.org/scholarships (July-November).

c) SAKSHAM SCHOLARSHIP FOR SPECIALLY ABLED (AICTE)
   - For specially-abled students including women.
   - Amount: Rs. 50,000 per year.
   - Eligibility: 40%+ disability; technical education; income < Rs. 8 lakh/year.

2. CENTRAL GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIPS
------------------------------------

a) POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP FOR SC/ST/OBC GIRLS
   - Amount: Varies by state; covers tuition, hostel, books, maintenance.
   - Eligibility: Post-matriculation studies; SC/ST/OBC category.
   - Apply: scholarships.gov.in

b) NATIONAL MEANS-CUM-MERIT SCHOLARSHIP (NMMSS)
   - For economically weak meritorious students (Class 9-12).
   - Amount: Rs. 12,000/year.
   - Eligibility: Family income < Rs. 3.5 lakh/year; 55%+ marks in previous class.

c) INSPIRE SCHOLARSHIP (DST)
   - For top 1% in Class 12 pursuing Natural Sciences.
   - Amount: Rs. 80,000/year for 5 years.
   - Includes mentorship from scientists.
   - Strong preference for women in STEM.

d) NATIONAL FELLOWSHIP AND SCHOLARSHIP FOR HIGHER EDUCATION OF ST STUDENTS
   - For ST women pursuing PG/PhD.
   - Full fellowship amount + contingency grant.

3. STATE GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIPS — KEY ONES
--------------------------------------------

a) KERALA — Dr. AMBEDKAR POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIP
   - Generous amounts for OBC/SC/ST women students.

b) MAHARASHTRA — RAJARSHI CHHATRAPATI SHAHU MAHARAJ MERIT SCHOLARSHIP
   - For economically backward class girls.
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 40,000/year for professional courses.

c) KARNATAKA — VIDYASIRI SCHOLARSHIP
   - For OBC/SC/ST students; covers full tuition for girl students.

d) DELHI — MUKHYAMANTRI VIGYAN PRATIBHA PURASKAR
   - For Class 9 girls with 60%+ marks; Rs. 5,000 one-time award.

e) WEST BENGAL — KANYASHREE PRAKALPA
   - Annual scholarship: Rs. 1,000 (Class 8-12 girls aged 13-18).
   - One-time grant: Rs. 25,000 (18+ unmarried girls in higher education).
   - Goal: Prevent child marriage, promote girls' education.

f) ANDHRA PRADESH/TELANGANA — JAGANANNA VIDYA DEEVENA
   - Full fee reimbursement for poor students in professional courses.

4. CORPORATE & FOUNDATION SCHOLARSHIPS FOR WOMEN
--------------------------------------------------

a) TATA CAPITAL PAG SCHOLARSHIP
   - For meritorious students from economically weak backgrounds.
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 12,000/year.
   - Apply: buddy4study.com

b) INFOSYS BPO SCHOLARSHIP
   - For IT and technical programs.
   - Preference to women candidates.

c) HDFC BANK PARIVARTAN SCHOLARSHIP
   - For undergraduate studies.
   - Amount: Up to Rs. 75,000.
   - Eligibility: Income < Rs. 2.5 lakh/year; 55%+ marks.

d) RELIANCE FOUNDATION SCHOLARSHIP
   - For undergraduate students.
   - Amount: Rs. 2 lakh/year.
   - Focus areas: Computer Science, Engineering, Economics, Social Sciences.

e) GOOGLE GENERATION SCHOLARSHIP — WOMEN IN TECH (ASIA PACIFIC)
   - For women pursuing Computer Science or related technical fields.
   - Amount: USD 1,000.
   - Mentorship from Google engineers.
   - Apply: buildyourfuture.withgoogle.com

f) ADOBE INDIA WOMEN-IN-TECHNOLOGY SCHOLARSHIP
   - For women pursuing Computer Science or related engineering degrees.
   - Amount: Rs. 75,000 per year for up to 4 years.
   - Benefits include mentorship and internship opportunities at Adobe.

g) AMAZON FUTURE ENGINEER SCHOLARSHIP
   - For students pursuing computer science.
   - Includes paid summer internship opportunity.

h) L'OREAL INDIA FOR YOUNG WOMEN IN SCIENCE
   - For women pursuing advanced studies in natural sciences.
   - Amount: Rs. 2.5 lakh one-time grant.
   - Eligibility: Must be enrolled in MSc/PhD; exceptional academic record.

5. INTERNATIONAL SCHOLARSHIPS ACCESSIBLE FROM INDIA
-----------------------------------------------------

a) FULBRIGHT-NEHRU MASTER'S FELLOWSHIPS (USA)
   - For Indian women/men to study master's in the US.
   - Covers tuition, travel, stipend, health insurance.
   - Apply: usief.org.in

b) COMMONWEALTH SCHOLARSHIPS (UK)
   - For postgraduate study in the UK.
   - Preference to women from developing countries.
   - Apply: cscuk.fcdo.gov.uk

c) AUSTRALIAN AWARDS SCHOLARSHIPS
   - For postgraduate study in Australia.
   - Covers tuition, airfare, living allowance.
   - Special focus on women, especially from rural areas.

d) DAAD SCHOLARSHIPS (GERMANY)
   - For postgraduate/PhD study in Germany.
   - Amount: EUR 861-1,200/month.
   - Apply: daad.de

e) ERASMUS MUNDUS JOINT MASTERS (EU)
   - Consortium of EU universities; fully funded.
   - Scholarship: EUR 1,400/month + travel + installation grant.

f) WOMEN TECHMAKERS SCHOLARSHIP (GOOGLE) — INDIA
   - For women in tech programs across India.
   - Community access, mentorship, scholarship grant.

6. SKILL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS (FREE/SUBSIDIZED)
-------------------------------------------------

a) PRADHAN MANTRI KAUSHAL VIKAS YOJANA (PMKVY)
   - Free short-term skill training in 300+ job roles.
   - Monetary reward on assessment & certification.
   - Available across India via training centers.
   - Apply: pmkvyofficial.org

b) SKILL INDIA — DIGI SAKSHAM
   - Free digital skills training for women.
   - Certification on completion.

c) NASSCOM FOUNDATION — FUTURE SKILLS
   - Free IT/digital skills training for women.
   - Job placement assistance.

d) COURSERA — INDIA SKILLS PROGRAM
   - Free access to 3,900+ courses for eligible students.
